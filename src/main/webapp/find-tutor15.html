About SkillCart

SkillCart is an affordable, student-centric e-commerce platform built to allow users to buy and sell skills easily. It operates like an online service marketplace but is specifically tailored for low-cost skill sharing, peer tutoring, and digital service booking.

Our main aim is to make skill access and delivery affordable, reliable, and easy to use, especially for students and early learners.

Unlike high-cost freelancing or learning platforms, SkillCart prioritizes low-budget accessibility and peer-to-peer interaction.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data to provide and improve our services:

  • User Information: Name, email address, mobile number, password
  • User Role Data: Whether you're registering as a Tutor or Learner
  • Service Details: Skills listed, prices, availability, and bookings
  • Messages: Chat content between Tutors and Learners (for safety and support)
  • Usage Info: Pages visited, time spent, actions taken (for analytics and improvements)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Match learners with relevant skill offers
  • Facilitate slot booking and service checkout
  • Enable secure messaging between users

  • Contact you for support or updates

We do not sell or share your personal information with third-party advertisers.

3. Data Security

We take your data privacy seriously by using:

  • Update or correct your details
  • Request account deletion
